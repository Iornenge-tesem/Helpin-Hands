Counselling

Individual Counseling
- One-on-one sessions with trained counselors to address personal issues, trauma, and emotional challenges - Support for residents to set and achieve personal goals, develop coping strategies, and improve mental health

Group Counseling
- Group therapy sessions focused on specific topics, such as anger management, self-esteem, or stress management - Support groups for residents to connect with others who have experienced similar challenges

Family Counseling
- Counseling sessions for families to address relationship dynamics, communication, and conflict resolution - Support for families to develop healthy boundaries, parenting skills, and emotional intelligence

Specialized Counseling
- Trauma-informed counseling for residents who have experienced abuse, neglect, or violence - Counseling for residents struggling with addiction, mental health issues, or other specialized concerns

Counseling Goals
- Empower residents to take control of their lives and make positive changes - Foster a sense of community and connection among residents - Provide residents with the tools and support needed to achieve stability, healing, and growth.
