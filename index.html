About

We Inspire to Aspire

Welcome to Helpin Hands We are a safe and supportive home shelter dedicated to providing a haven for individuals and families in need. Our mission is to offer a comprehensive range of services that foster stability, healing, and growth. Donate Again!

Services

Relief

Emotional Support: Providing a listening ear and emotional support to help residents cope with stress, anxiety, and trauma.
- Basic Needs Assistance: Offering essential items such as food, clothing, and toiletries to help resident meet their basic needs.

Counselling

Individual Counseling - One-on-one sessions with trained counselors to address personal issues, trauma, and emotional challenges - Support for residents to set and achieve personal goals, develop coping strategies, and improve mental health.

Skills acquisition

Vocational Skills
- Training in areas such as:
- Carpentry and woodworking
- Sewing and tailoring
- Cooking and baking
- Hairdressing and beauty services
- Automotive repair and maintenance.

Education

Academic Support - Tutoring and homework assistance for children and youth
- Support for residents to earn their GED or high school diploma
- Assistance with online coursework and educational resources
