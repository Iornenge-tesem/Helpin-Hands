ABOUT

Welcome to Helpin Hands
We are a safe and supportive home shelter dedicated to providing a haven for individuals and families in need. Our mission is to offer a comprehensive range of services that foster stability, healing, and growth.

Our Vision
We envision a community where everyone has access to safe and affordable housing, nutritious food, and opportunities for education and personal growth. We strive to create a warm and inclusive environment that promotes dignity, respect, and hope.

Our Services
We offer a range of services designed to support the physical, emotional, and social well-being of our residents. These include: - Emergency shelter and housing - Food and nutrition programs - Counseling and mental health services - Education and job training programs - Health and wellness services - Case management and advocacy

Our Values
We are guided by a set of core values that reflect our commitment to serving our residents with compassion, respect, and dignity. These values include: - Hospitality: We welcome everyone with open arms and a warm smile. - Respect: We treat every individual with dignity and respect. - Empowerment: We empower our residents to take control of their lives and make positive changes. - Community: We foster a sense of community and belonging among our residents.

Get Involved
We invite you to join us in our mission to provide a safe and supportive home for those in need. Whether you'd like to volunteer, donate, or spread the word, every effort counts!
