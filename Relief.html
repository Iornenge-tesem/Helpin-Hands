Relief

- Emotional Support: Providing a listening ear and emotional support to help residents cope with stress, anxiety, and trauma.

- Basic Needs Assistance: Offering essential items such as food, clothing, and toiletries to help residents meet their basic needs.

- Crisis Intervention: Providing immediate support and intervention during times of crisis, such as domestic violence or mental health emergencies.

- Respite Care: Offering temporary relief and support to caregivers, allowing them to take a break and recharge.

- Referral Services: Connecting residents with external resources and services, such as counseling, medical care, and financial assistance.
